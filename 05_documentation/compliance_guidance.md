# Compliance Guidance

This document describes the regulatory and safety rules applied during an ethics audit.

---

## 1. Regulatory Checks
- GDPR minimal requirements
- CCPA disclosures
- Consent verification
- Data minimization
- Right-to-explanation (model transparency)

---

## 2. Risk Classes
- High risk → PII leaks, discrimination
- Medium risk → unclear intent, ambiguous features
- Low risk → explainable, non-sensitive usage

---

## 3. Required Evidence
- Consent metadata
- Model documentation
- Data lineage maps
- Preprocessing logs
