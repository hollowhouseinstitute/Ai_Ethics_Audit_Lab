# Pricing — AI Ethics Audit Lab

## Principles
Pricing reflects scope, risk exposure, and interpretive depth.
No pricing implies certification or regulatory approval.

## Tier 1 — Research Audit
Typical Use: Internal exploration, early-stage assessment
Deliverables: Executive Summary, limited findings, core scoring
Turnaround: 5–7 business days
Price Range: USD 3,500 – 6,000

## Tier 2 — Commercial Audit
Typical Use: Pre-release readiness, internal governance
Deliverables: Full findings report, scoring matrix, evidence index
Turnaround: 7–10 business days
Price Range: USD 8,000 – 15,000

## Tier 3 — Enterprise Audit
Typical Use: High-stakes systems, executive oversight
Deliverables: Full audit + custom risk lenses + executive briefing
Turnaround: 10–15 business days
Price Range: USD 18,000 – 35,000

## Notes
- Pricing varies by model complexity and documentation maturity
- Ongoing or comparative audits are quoted separately
